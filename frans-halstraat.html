Frans Halstraat
Mission:
Find a street in the Frans Halstraat neighorbood in De Pijp. Talk to someone about why there are so many plants growing in car parking spaces and what do they think about the initiative?
De Pijp is a vibrant neighborhood located in the southern part of Amsterdam, known for its lively atmosphere, multiculturalism, and variety of restaurants and cafes. It's home to the famous Albert Cuyp Market and the picturesque Sarphatipark.
